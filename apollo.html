Mapping Apollo Landing Sites

This interactive map presents a streamlined version of two QGIS maps originally created to showcase Apollo landing sites on the Moon. Due to QGIS2Web plugin limitations, which prevent integration of lunar base maps via the required WMS server, the base maps are not included in this adaptation.

The map features two key layers: one illustrating the major lunar maria (vast, dark plains on the Moonâ€™s surface) and another pinpointing the Apollo mission landing sites. Hover over a landing site to view pop-up details, including crew information and mission dates. A search feature allows you to locate sites by mission name. Zooming in or out may reveal additional names of notable lunar maria.

For a more comprehensive look at the original QGIS project that maps the Apollo landing sites, including the base maps, visit the project's dedicated webpage.
