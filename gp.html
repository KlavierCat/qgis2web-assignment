Mapping GPs distribution in Ireland and the EU

The map, GPs in Ireland, visualizes the distribution of General Practitioners (GPs) across Ireland by county. It includes three distinct data layers, each representing a different aspect of GP distribution, which you can toggle on and off using the layer control in the interactive map. The three layers are:

  • GPs per 100,000 residents by county
  • GPs per 100 square kilometers by county
  • Total number of GPs by county

The data was aggregated and processed using Node.js.

For comparison, the map GPs in the EU provides an overview of GP distribution across Europe, showing the number of GPs per 100,000 residents by country.

This page is responsive: on larger screens, the two maps are displayed side by side; on smaller screens, they stack vertically for easier viewing.
