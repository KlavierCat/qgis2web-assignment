Mapping lighthouses in the Irish Isles

The Irish Isles are home to a remarkable collection of lighthouses, each with its own unique history and charm. This map presents the lighthouses of both the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland, using data from OpenStreetMap. When you hover over an icon on the map, a pop-up will appear, displaying details about each lighthouse.

If you're interested in exploring some of Ireland's iconic lighthouses—or even staying in one for a holiday—you can find more information on the following websites:

  • Great Lighthouses of Ireland – Discover Ireland’s iconic lighthouses and nearby attractions.
  • Commissioners of Irish Lights – Learn about lighthouse operations and maritime safety in Ireland.
  • Irish Landmark Trust – Book a stay in a restored historic lighthouse.
